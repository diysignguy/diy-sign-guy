Sign Kit Questions

Sign Kit FAQ

Are the kits UL listed?

No. DIY Sign Guy cannot UL list a DIY kit. Please check with your local township and follow all applicable sign, zoning, electrical, inspection and installation guidelines before placing your order.

Can the same frame be double-sided?

Yes. The same frame can support a single-sided or double-sided configuration.

How is a big sign shipped?

Kits ship in four sections; larger kits may use six sections with splice joints to help avoid freight when package dimensions allow.
