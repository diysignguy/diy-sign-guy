Return & Refund Policy

Local Codes & Permits. Sign size, height, placement, and lighting rules vary by city, county, and township. It is the buyer's responsibility to confirm local codes and permit requirements before ordering. DIY Sign Guy is not liable for code violations, and we do not issue refunds for signs that do not meet local code requirements — please check with your local permitting office before you order.

Damaged in Shipping. If your sign arrives damaged, please refuse the delivery rather than accepting it, and contact us right away so we can make it right.

14-Day Returns. If you receive your sign and decide it isn't right for you, you may return it within 14 days of delivery. Contact us to arrange the return.

Assembly Safety. These kits include metal components with sharp edges. Always wear eye protection, gloves, and appropriate safety equipment during assembly.

Electrical & Installation. These kits are built for the DIYer, but electrical work and mounting should be performed or reviewed by a licensed electrician and/or licensed installer in your area. DIY Sign Guy is not responsible for issues resulting from electrical work or installation performed by unlicensed individuals.
